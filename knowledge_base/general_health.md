# General Health & When to See a Doctor

## Common Symptoms and What They Might Mean

### Fever
**What it is**: Body temperature above 98.6°F (37°C)

**What to do**:
- Below 102°F (38.9°C): Rest, fluids, paracetamol if uncomfortable
- Above 102°F: Monitor closely, see doctor if lasts more than 3 days
- Above 103°F (39.4°C): See doctor immediately

**When to worry**:
- Lasts more than 3 days
- With severe headache, stiff neck, or rash
- With difficulty breathing
- In infants under 3 months (any fever is an emergency)

### Headache
**Common causes**: Stress, dehydration, lack of sleep, eye strain, tension

**Home care**:
- Drink water
- Rest in quiet, dark room
- Cold or warm compress on head/neck
- Over-the-counter pain reliever

**See doctor if**:
- Sudden, severe headache ("worst of your life")
- After head injury
- With fever, confusion, or stiff neck
- With weakness, numbness, or vision changes
- Gets progressively worse over days

### Cough
**Types**:
- Dry cough: No mucus, often viral
- Wet cough: Produces mucus, can be infection

**Home relief**:
- Honey (1-2 teaspoons, not for children under 1)
- Warm fluids (tea, soup)
- Steam inhalation
- Throat lozenges
- Stay hydrated

**See doctor if**:
- Lasts more than 3 weeks
- With high fever
- Coughing up blood
- With shortness of breath
- Whooping sound when breathing in

### Body Pain
**Common causes**: Viral infections, overexertion, stress, poor sleep

**What helps**:
- Rest
- Warm bath or shower
- Gentle stretching
- Paracetamol or ibuprofen
- Massage

**See doctor if**:
- Severe pain without obvious cause
- With swelling or redness
- After injury
- Lasts more than a week
- With fever

### Stomach Issues

#### Nausea/Vomiting:
- Sip clear fluids slowly
- Ginger tea or candies
- Rest with head elevated
- Avoid solid food until vomiting stops

#### Diarrhea:
- Drink ORS (oral rehydration solution)
- Eat bland foods (bananas, rice, toast)
- Avoid dairy, spicy, fatty foods
- See doctor if lasts more than 2 days or has blood

#### Stomach Pain:
- Rest and avoid eating for a few hours
- Sip warm water
- Don't take painkillers (can worsen some conditions)
- See doctor if severe, persistent, or with fever

## When to Go to Emergency Room

### Call Emergency Services (108 in India) or Go to ER:
- Chest pain or pressure
- Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
- Sudden severe headache
- Confusion or difficulty speaking
- Weakness or numbness on one side of body
- Severe allergic reaction (swelling, difficulty breathing)
- Uncontrolled bleeding
- Severe burns
- Loss of consciousness
- Seizures
- Suicidal thoughts

### When to See a Doctor Soon (Within 24-48 Hours):
- Fever lasting more than 3 days
- Persistent vomiting or diarrhea
- Severe sore throat with fever
- Ear pain
- Painful urination
- Rash with fever
- Symptoms that improve then return worse

## General Health Tips

### Daily Habits:
- **Sleep**: 7-9 hours per night
- **Water**: 8-10 glasses daily
- **Exercise**: 30 minutes moderate activity most days
- **Handwashing**: Frequently with soap for 20 seconds
- **Balanced diet**: Fruits, vegetables, whole grains, protein

### Boost Your Immunity:
- Eat colorful fruits and vegetables
- Get adequate vitamin D (sunlight or supplements)
- Manage stress through meditation, yoga, or hobbies
- Don't smoke
- Limit alcohol
- Stay up to date with vaccinations

### Mental Health Matters:
- Talk to someone if feeling overwhelmed
- Practice relaxation techniques
- Maintain social connections
- Seek professional help for persistent sadness or anxiety
- It's okay to ask for help

## Home Medicine Kit Essentials:
- Paracetamol (fever, pain)
- ORS packets (dehydration)
- Bandages and antiseptic
- Thermometer
- Antihistamine (allergies)
- Cough syrup
- Antacid
- Any prescription medications you regularly need

## Important Reminders:
- Don't self-diagnose serious conditions
- Don't ignore persistent symptoms
- Don't take antibiotics without prescription
- Do complete full medication courses
- Do get regular health check-ups
- Do trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, get it checked
